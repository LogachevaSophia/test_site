History
ONIGIRI

Carbonized clumps of rice, similar to onigiri, have been discovered in late Yayoi Period ruins, indicating that food like onigiri has been in existence since then. However, the onigiri-like food at that time seemed to be rice wrapped in bamboo leaves in a corn-like shape, and boiled or steamed. The origin of the present form of onigiri dates back to the Heian Period, when there was a food called “Ton-jiki.’
